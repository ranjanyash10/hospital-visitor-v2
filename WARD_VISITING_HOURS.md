---

> [!NOTE]
> **Visiting Policy**
> - Only **one visitor** is allowed per patient during these sessions.
> - Visitors must have a valid digital slip or QR code.
> - Children below 12 years are generally not allowed in ICU zones.

> [!TIP]
> **Fast-Track Entry**
> Registration can be done via the **Walk-In Kiosk** at the main entrance or through the **WhatsApp Portal** to save time at the gate.
